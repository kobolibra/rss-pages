Plan Sponsor Survey 2026

Mon Feb 02 2026 11:40:00 GMT-0600
In our annual Retirement Survey & Insights Report, our participant-focused research has consistently identified competing financial priorities as a growing reality making retirement savings affordability a central concern for savers.

Plan sponsors are pivotal to America’s retirement system, significantly influencing how millions of individuals save and prepare for their financial futures. Therefore, we also surveyed retirement plan sponsors to understand how they approach the challenges faced by their employees, and what benefits and plan design enhancements they may be considering to ensure their employees can reach retirement prepared.

Key Findings

Plan Sponsor Perspectives

Some plan sponsors possess diverse perspectives on the function of retirement plans within their overall employee benefits packages, with a holistic approach representing the most prevalent choice. Others focus solely on retirement savings or benchmarking against competitors. Plan sponsors we surveyed believe that only 33% of their employees are on track for retirement.

Investment Menu Innovation

Plan sponsor respondent’s top considerations for the next 12 months include enhancing inflation protection options, adding personalized investment solutions like managed accounts, and integrating private market strategies. These insights signal a clear focus towards more sophisticated, personalized, and protective investment solutions for retirement plan participants.

Retirement Income Remains a Top Consideration

Retirement income seems to continue to be a complex challenge for the industry, with a growing number of plan sponsors considering ways to offer solutions that extend beyond the accumulation phase. Among plan sponsors surveyed, the top objective is to provide income that is guaranteed for life relative to other stable income or growth-oriented options.

Advice and Managed Accounts

Access to personalized managed accounts continues to grow, with 75% of plans offering this service. Plan sponsors aim to prioritize features like custom configuration to align with key plan features, a broad spectrum of retirement planning and savings advice, and participant engagement.
